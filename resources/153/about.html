A Free Armenian News Text Corpus, provided by Qaghaki Amsagir LLC (Yerevan City Magazine, evnmag.com)

Yerevan City Magazine (evnmag) has been published since 2011. Its main focus is the city life, urban problems and citizens of Yerevan. Each issue of the magazine is dedicated to one main topic (public transport, urban activism, COVID lockdown, etc). Different formats of text are being used: from essays and reports to interviews and monologues, always using modern, lively Armenian language. Content of the corpus includes articles published in the magazine from 2017 to 2021.
