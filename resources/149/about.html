This dataset consists of 100 common Tibetan greetings, carefully selected and recorded in a relatively quiet environment.

It includes 30 native Tibetan speakers, with 10 speakers each from the Kham, Amdo, and U-Tsang dialect regions. In the dataset, each speaker has recordings of 100 greetings, categorized by dialect region.

Collected, organized, and produced by undergraduate students Linfei Lu, Jiaxin Pang, Stansencuo, Buwonglam, and Linting Huang from Minzu University of China.
